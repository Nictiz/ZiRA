Discussing and determining diagnosis, treatment plan and/or treatment in a joint discussion of healthcare providers from different disciplines in order to be able to offer the best possible appropriate treatment to the patient and to guarantee the quality of care. This process can take place within a single organization, but also with specialized expertise from multiple centers (echeloning). MDOs usually take place periodically (e.g. weekly) and pre-planned. <br/>
