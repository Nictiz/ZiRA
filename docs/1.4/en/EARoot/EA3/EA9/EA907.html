Medication supply describes the supply of a medicinal product to a specific patient (or the administerer or a representative), with the intention of it being used according to the included instructions (usually to honor the supply request in a medication prescription).  <br/>Medication supply takes place when the patient (or any of the others listed above) physically receives the product.<br/>
