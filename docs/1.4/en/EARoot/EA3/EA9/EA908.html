A medication prescription is an agreement or order for the use of medication in which the following are described: the prescribed product, instructions for use or administration and (optionally) a request for delivery. <br/>The dose information is given for every prescribed product: the start date and time, and if possible stop date and time or total number of administrations, administering schedule (frequency or interval, administering times, link with meals etc.), the number of doses, administering speed or time (for drips), and the route of administration.  <br/>An indication can also be included of whether the medication is only to be administered 'as needed' and under which conditions the product is to be used, and how high the agreed maximum dose is in a certain period.  <br/>If possible, the reason or indication of starting/stopping/changes in use is to be included. <br/>Medication that has been discontinued temporarily can be included as well. <br/>If the prescription also contains the order to pr<br/>
