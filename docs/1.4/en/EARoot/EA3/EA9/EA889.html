<font color="#ff0000"><b>! Attention: this information model is undergoing major revision. A new version will be available after summer.</b></font><br/>Important parts of the brain's general mental functions are intellectual functions, orientation and consciousness.  <br/>If there is a disorder in the intellectual functions as a result of a mental disability, it is not described in this information model but is recorded as a medical diagnosis. <br/>Disorders in orientation and consciousness combined with disorders in attention, memory and observation are seen as important symptoms for the potential presence of a delirium. The latter disorders are recorded in the SpecificMentalFunctions information model.<br/>
