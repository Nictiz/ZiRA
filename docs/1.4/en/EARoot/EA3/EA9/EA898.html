A drip is a machine that slowly injects fluid into a blood vessel. The drip has a number of components. <br/>The drip contains: <br/><ul>
<li>the (peripheral) cannula or the (central) catheter put into the patient peripherally or centrally;</li><li>the administering system connected to the cannula/catheter allowing the administered fluid to run;</li><li>the drip bag containing the fluid. </li></ul>
<br/>Multiple administering systems may be connected to one cannula/catheter. Furthermore, a central venous catheter may have multiple lumens. <br/>Arterial and epidural catheters also exist in addition to venous catheters.<br/>
