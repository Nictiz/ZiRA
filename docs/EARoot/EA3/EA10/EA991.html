It is a part of the nursing process, together with nursing diagnoses/problems, the treatment goal and nursing interventions. <br/>There are different classification systems that describe healthcare results with a measurement value, such as the NOC (Nursing Outcome Classification), Omaha System, and ICF (International Classification of Functioning, Disability and Health).
